Why Monitor the Wasatch?

The Wasatch Front faces significant environmental changes, including air pollution, water scarcity, and deforestation. Our goal is to analyze these changes and provide real data.

NDVI Trends Over Time

We analyze the vegetation health using NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index). This helps us track the impact of climate change.

Air Quality & Temperature Changes

As temperatures rise, air quality worsens due to increased wildfires and pollution. Our data visualizations reveal these trends.
